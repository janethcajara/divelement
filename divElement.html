Euthenics 2--CCC Leader Development and Governance

Euthenics is a branch of art and science that deals with the improvement of human functioning, efficiency, and well-being by modifying controllable environmental factors such as living conditions and education. The word euthenics is derived from the Greek word euthenein, which means, “to thrive or flourish.”

GE_4--Purposive Communication

Purposive communication is a form of communication that is planned and tailored for different purposes and audiences. It involves using various modes of communication, such as writing, speaking, and presenting, to effectively and efficiently relay concepts and ideas across cultural and philosophical lines. Purposive communication also requires thinking about the reason, the approach, and the outcome of the communication.

PATHFIT 2--Exercise-based Fitness Activity

Exercise is a structured program of activity geared toward achieving or maintaining physical fitness. It is actually a sub-category of physical activity. And physical activity is any form of exercise or movement of the body that uses energy.
